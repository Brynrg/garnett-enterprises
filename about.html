Meet the Team

At Garnett Enterprises, we are dedicated to acquiring and managing businesses with precision and expertise. Our leadership team brings decades of combined experience in operational excellence, strategic planning, and financial management. We understand the complexities of running and growing a business, and we take a methodical approach to ensuring long-term success.

Brynr Garnett

Brynr excels in strategic planning and operational execution, ensuring that businesses thrive under his leadership. His ability to scale operations, implement innovative solutions, and foster sustainable business growth makes him an invaluable asset in any acquisition.

James Rydjeski, CPA

James brings a strong financial and analytical background to Garnett Enterprises. As a practicing CPA with a Master's degree in Accounting from BYU's Marriott School of Business, as well as experience at large companies like Deloitte and SAIC, he has developed deep expertise in financial planning, risk management, and internal controls.

James's keen eye for financial health and strategic planning ensures that every business acquired by Garnett Enterprises operates with fiscal strength and long-term viability. His analytical prowess and leadership in financial management provide a strong foundation for sustained business success.
